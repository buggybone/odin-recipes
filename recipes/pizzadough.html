Pizza Dough

This recipe will describe how to make a pizza dough

Cook the dough on a grill with sauce and cheese for a real crowd pleaser.

  • 1 tbsp yeast
  • 3 cups flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • Sugar
  • Olive Oil
  1. Add the yeast to a bowl of hot-tub temperature water and a spoonful of suger
  2. Meanwhile, pour the flour into a bowl with a pinch of salt, some sugar, and a tbsp of olive oil.
  3. After a few minutes, the yeast bowl should be bubbling. If not, repeat step one with cooler water or new yeast.
  4. If the yeast is bubbling, pour it into the bowl with the flour.
  5. Mix thoroughly and let rise in a warm place for 2 hours.
  6. Knead the dough, then let rise in the refrigerator until you're ready to use it.
