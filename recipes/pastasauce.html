Pasta Sauce

This recipe will describe how to make a marinara sauce

Serve with al dente spaghetti noodles and parmesan cheese.

  • Onion
  • One large can of San Marzano tomato sauce
  • One small can of diced tomatos
  • Half a stick of butter
  • Salt
  1. Peel the onion and chop it in half.
  2. Pour the two cans of tomatoes into a pot and heat on medium.
  3. Add the onion halves and butter into the pot.
  4. Simmer until the desired consistency is reached.
  5. Season with salt to taste.
  6. Remove and toss the onion halves.
  7. Serve over spaghetti noodles.
