Taco Recipe

This recipe will describe how to make a marinade for flank steak tacos.

Serve the meat with your favorite salsa on a corn tortilla for a delicious meal.

  • Cilantro
  • Oranges
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Jalapeno
  • Serrano Chile
  • Flank Steak
  1. Juice the oranges and limes into a plastic bag.
  2. Finely chop the cilatro and add to the bag.
  3. Mince the garlic into the bag.
  4. Finely dice the chiles and add them to the bag.
  5. Season with salt.
  6. Add olive oil until the marinade has doubled in volume.
  7. Add the flank steak to the bag and marinade overnight.
  8. Grill the steak.
